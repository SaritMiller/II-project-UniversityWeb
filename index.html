Worlds best university of Architecture

Welcome to KTH ROYAL, we offer nine master's programmes within the subject area Architecture and the Built Environment that addresses different aspects of the development of future societies. For example, they cover designing and building cities, infrastructure and buildings, advancing sustainable transport systems and developing regulatory systems that create healthy and safe living environments. KTH is ranked in the top 10 among all universities in the world within the area.
